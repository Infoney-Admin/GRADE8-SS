Activity Introduction

Every solution begins with an idea. In this activity, you will drag possible solutions into the correct problem area. By the end, you will see how different ideas match different community challenges.

Learner Instructions

Drag each solution card into the problem box that it best addresses. After placing each card, read the feedback to understand why it is correct or incorrect.

Here is a hint

  • Look at the problem carefully and think about the need it creates.
  • A solution for health may not be the same as one for education.
  • One problem may have more than one correct solution.
