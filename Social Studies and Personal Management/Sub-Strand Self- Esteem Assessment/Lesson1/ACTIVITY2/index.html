Activity Introduction

Self-esteem is at the centre of holistic growth. In this activity, you will explore a wheel that shows how it strengthens every part of your life.

Learner Instructions

  • Click each section of the wheel to begin.
  • Read the explanation and look at the example.
  • Think about how self-esteem appears in your own life.
  • Keep clicking until you've explored all five areas!

Helpful Hints...

  • Which trait helps you speak up in class?
  • Which area helps you keep going after failure?
  • Which area of life benefits when you manage your emotions well?
