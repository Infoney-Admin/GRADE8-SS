Activity Introduction

Personality researchers use five key traits to understand people. In this activity, you will spin a wheel and learn about each one. You will also see examples that connect to your own life.

Learner Instructions

  • Spin the wheel to land on a personality trait.
  • Read the definition and look at the example.
  • Ask yourself: Do I see this trait in my daily life?
  • Keep spinning until you've learned about all five!

Helpful Hints...

  • Openness: enjoying new experiences and ideas.
  • Conscientiousness: being responsible and organised.
  • Neuroticism: experiencing stress or strong emotions sometimes.
  • Extroversion: being sociable and confident.
  • Agreeableness: being kind and cooperative.
